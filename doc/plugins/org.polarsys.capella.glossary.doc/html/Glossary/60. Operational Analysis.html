OPERATIONAL ANALYSIS

DEFINITION

Before and beyond System/Software requirements, Operational Analysis focuses on the customer needs and goals. Its objective is to ensure good adequacy of System/Software definition with regards to its real operational use.

Outputs of Operational Analysis consist mainly in an ‘operational architecture’ describing and structuring this need in terms of Actors/Users, their Operational Capabilities and Activities, operational use Scenarios giving dimensioning parameters, operational constraints including safety, security, system life cycle….
